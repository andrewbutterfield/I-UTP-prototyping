\textbf{Drawn from email from Jim Woodcock, June 15th 2026}

An institution is an abstract account of a logic. Here, we use a form with:

\begin{itemize}

\item a category $\clz \Sig$ of signatures and signature morphisms;

\item a sentence functor $\clz \Sen : \Sig \to \mathbf{Set}$;
\item a model functor $\clz \Mod : \Sig^{op} \to \mathbf{Cat}$;
\item for each signature $\clz \Sigma$, a satisfaction relation
      $\clz \models_{\Sigma} \subseteq |\Mod(\Sigma)| \times \Sen(\Sigma)$.
\end{itemize}

The satisfaction condition links the four components. For a signature morphism $\clz \sigma: \Sigma \to \Sigma'$,
for a sentence $\clz \varphi \in \Sen(\Sigma)$, and for a model $\clz M' \in |\Mod(\Sigma')|$,

\begin{equation}\clz
  M' \models_{\Sigma'} \Sen(\sigma)(\varphi)
  \quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
  \Mod(\sigma)(M') \models_{\Sigma} \varphi .
\end{equation}



  \centering

  \[
    \begin{tikzcd}[column sep=huge,row sep=large]
      \varphi \in \operatorname{Sen}(\Sigma)
      \arrow[r, "{\operatorname{Sen}(\sigma)}"]
      &
      \operatorname{Sen}(\sigma)(\varphi) \in \operatorname{Sen}(\Sigma') \\
      \operatorname{Mod}(\sigma)(M') \in |\operatorname{Mod}(\Sigma)|
      \arrow[u, dashed, "\models_{\Sigma}"']
      &
      M' \in |\operatorname{Mod}(\Sigma')|
      \arrow[l, "{\operatorname{Mod}(\sigma)}"']
      \arrow[u, dashed, "\models_{\Sigma'}"]
    \end{tikzcd}
  \]
 
  \medskip
 
  \small
  \textbf{The satisfaction condition.}
 
  Translating a sentence forward along \(\sigma : \Sigma \to \Sigma'\) and reducing a model backward along \(\sigma\) preserve satisfaction:
  \[
    M' \models_{\Sigma'} \operatorname{Sen}(\sigma)(\varphi)
    \quad \Longleftrightarrow \quad
    \operatorname{Mod}(\sigma)(M') \models_{\Sigma} \varphi .
  \]
